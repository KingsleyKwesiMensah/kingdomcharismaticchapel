About Us

Apostle Dominic Adu-Boahen
(Founder, Kingdom Charismatic Chapel )

Kingdom Charismatic Chapel (K.C.C) is an evangelical charismatic church founded in November, 2011 by the Apostle Dominic Adu-Boahen.
He moved to Takoradi after graduating from the University of Cape Coast to continue service as a professional teacher.
He obeyed the voice of God to start a church after having served as patron for the Scripture Union at Ghana Secondary Technical School (G.S.T.S).
The apostle used to organise few students for prayer meeting during vacation and through that he declared his intention to them about establishing a church
and thanks be to God few of the students joined him together with his wife and few others from the community gathered on one Sunday in November to have the first service.
